scroll to begin ↓
  1. 01

    Non-contact radar

    The Rosemount 5900S fires a microwave signal at the product surface and times the echo — ±0.5 mm, custody-transfer approved (OIML R85). Nothing touches the liquid.

  2. 02

    Temperature & mass

    The 2240S reads up to 16 temperature points down the tank, so the system corrects volume and computes true mass — radar + temperature + pressure, hybrid density.

  3. 03

    One smart hub

    The 2410 Tank Hub powers and connects every field device on the tank over one intrinsically-safe bus — with independent relay outputs for overfill alarms.

  4. 04

    The whole farm, one wire

    The 2460 System Hub polls every tank and concentrates the data, presenting the entire terminal to host systems over open protocols.

  5. 05

    Into the control room

    The signal arrives at TankMaster — live inventory, custody reports and alarms for the whole terminal, in one window.

  6. 06

    The whole site, in your pocket

    TankMaster Mobile takes that same live data out of the control room — every tank on the site, real-time, on any phone or tablet, wherever the work happens.

  7. 07

    Every site, one network

    Zoom out and the picture connects: multiple terminals streaming up to HQ and authorised external users — regional inventory, balances and alarms across the whole operation, securely in one place.
